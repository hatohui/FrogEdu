SEMESTER 1: UNITS 1 – 10
Unit 1: Hello
Phonics: /h/ (Hello), /b/ (Bye)

Vocabulary: Hello, Hi, goodbye, bye, fine, thank you.

Sentence Pattern: Hello, I'm (Nam). / How are you? - I'm fine, thank you.

Unit 2: Our names
Phonics: /m/ (My), /n/ (Name)

Vocabulary: Name, what, your, spell.

Sentence Pattern: What's your name? - My name's (Mary). / How do you spell your name?

Unit 3: Our friends
Phonics: /t/ (This), /t/ (That)

Vocabulary: Mr, Ms, friend, teacher, Tony, Mary.

Sentence Pattern: This is (Mr Long). / Is that (Mary)? - Yes, it is. / No, it isn't.

Unit 4: Our bodies
Phonics: /f/ (Face), /h/ (Hand)

Vocabulary: Face, hand, ear, eye, nose, mouth, head.

Sentence Pattern: Touch your (face). / Open your (mouth).

Unit 5: My hobbies
Phonics: /s/ (Singing), /d/ (Dancing)

Vocabulary: Singing, dancing, cooking, drawing, swimming, running, painting.

Sentence Pattern: What's your hobby? - It's (singing). / I like (dancing).

Review 1 & Extension Activities
Unit 6: Our school
Phonics: /s/ (School), /l/ (Library)

Vocabulary: School, classroom, library, playground, gym, music room, art room.

Sentence Pattern: Let's go to the (library). / Is our school big? - Yes, it is.

Unit 7: Classroom instructions
Phonics: /c/ (Come), /s/ (Sit)

Vocabulary: Come in, go out, sit down, stand up, speak, open, close.

Sentence Pattern: May I (come in)? - Yes, you can. / No, you can't.

Unit 8: My school things
Phonics: /p/ (Pen), /r/ (Ruler)

Vocabulary: Pen, pencil, ruler, rubber, notebook, book, school bag, pencil case.

Sentence Pattern: I have a (pen). / Do you have a (ruler)? - Yes, I do.

Unit 9: Colours
Phonics: /b/ (Blue), /bl/ (Black)

Vocabulary: Red, blue, green, yellow, orange, white, brown.

Sentence Pattern: What colour is it? - It's (red). / What colour are they? - They're (blue).

Unit 10: Break time activities
Phonics: /p/ (Play), /f/ (Football)

Vocabulary: Football, basketball, badminton, chess, table tennis, hide-and-seek.

Sentence Pattern: What do you do at break time? - I play (football).

SEMESTER 2: UNITS 11 – 20
Unit 11: My family
Phonics: /f/ (Father), /m/ (Mother)

Vocabulary: Father, mother, brother, sister, grandmother, grandfather.

Sentence Pattern: Who's that? - That's my (father). / He's/She's (young).

Unit 12: Jobs
Phonics: /t/ (Teacher), /d/ (Doctor)

Vocabulary: Teacher, doctor, nurse, driver, worker, farmer, pupil.

Sentence Pattern: What's his/her job? - He's/She's a (doctor).

Unit 13: My house
Phonics: /k/ (Kitchen), /l/ (Living room)

Vocabulary: Living room, bedroom, kitchen, bathroom, dining room, garden.

Sentence Pattern: Where's the (living room)? - It's here/there.

Unit 14: My bedroom
Phonics: /b/ (Bed), /d/ (Desk)

Vocabulary: Bed, desk, chair, door, window, wardrobe.

Sentence Pattern: There's a (bed) in the room. / There are two (chairs) in the room.

Unit 15: At the dining table
Phonics: /m/ (Milk), /j/ (Juice)

Vocabulary: Milk, juice, water, bread, rice, meat, fish, beans.

Sentence Pattern: Would you like some (milk)? - Yes, please. / No, thank you.

Unit 16: My pets
Phonics: /d/ (Dog), /c/ (Cat)

Vocabulary: Dog, cat, bird, parrot, rabbit, goldfish, hamster.

Sentence Pattern: Do you have any (cats)? - Yes, I do. / I have (three) cats.

Unit 17: Our toys
Phonics: /k/ (Kite), /sh/ (Ship)

Vocabulary: Car, doll, ball, teddy bear, kite, ship, plane, yo-yo.

Sentence Pattern: How many (cars) do you have? - I have (two).

Unit 18: Playing and learning
Phonics: /r/ (Reading), /wr/ (Writing)

Vocabulary: Reading, writing, listening, speaking, counting, spelling.

Sentence Pattern: What are they doing? - They're (reading).

Unit 19: Outdoor activities
Phonics: /sk/ (Skating), /c/ (Cycling)

Vocabulary: Skating, cycling, flying a kite, skipping, sliding.

Sentence Pattern: What is he/she doing? - He's/She's (cycling).

Unit 20: At the zoo
Phonics: /m/ (Monkey), /t/ (Tiger)

Vocabulary: Monkey, tiger, elephant, hippo, kangaroo, crocodile.

Sentence Pattern: What's that animal? - It's a (monkey). / I like (elephants).
