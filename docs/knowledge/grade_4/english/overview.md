Dưới đây là bản tổng hợp chi tiết nội dung **Tiếng Anh 4 Global Success - Tập 1** bằng tiếng Anh, phân tích cụ thể các hoạt động mà học sinh sẽ thực hiện trong từng bài học (Unit 1 - Unit 10).

---

## Unit 1 – My friends

**Topic:** Countries and Nationalities.

- **Vocabulary:** _America, Australia, Britain, Japan, Thailand, Vietnam._
- **Sentence Patterns:**
- Asking about hometown: _Where are you from? -> I'm from [Country]._
- Asking about someone else: _Where is he/she from? -> He's/She's from [Country]._

- **Detailed Activities:**
- **Map Identification:** Students look at world maps and flags to identify different countries.
- **Role-play:** Pair up to act as international students meeting for the first time at a summer camp.
- **Phonics:** Practicing word stress on country names like _A'merica_ and _Aus'tralia_.

---

## Unit 2 – Time and daily routines

**Topic:** Telling time and everyday activities.

- **Vocabulary:** _get up, have breakfast, go to school, go to bed, o'clock, fifteen, thirty, forty-five._
- **Sentence Patterns:**
- Asking the time: _What time is it? -> It's [time]._
- Asking about routines: _What time do you [activity]? -> I [activity] at [time]._

- **Detailed Activities:**
- **Clock Drawing:** Draw hands on clock faces to match the time mentioned in the listening tasks.
- **Daily Log:** Create a simple table showing personal daily schedules and share them with a partner.
- **Game:** "What's the time, Mr. Wolf?" to practice speaking time out loud.

---

## Unit 3 – My week

**Topic:** Days of the week and recurring activities.

- **Vocabulary:** _Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday, Sunday, go to school, listen to music, help my parents, visit my grandparents._
- **Sentence Patterns:**
- Asking for the day: _What day is it today? -> It's [Day]._
- Asking about weekly activities: _What do you do on [Day]s? -> I [activity]._

- **Detailed Activities:**
- **Calendar Filling:** Fill in a blank weekly calendar with specific hobbies or chores.
- **Interview:** Ask classmates "What do you do on Saturdays?" and record their answers to find common interests.
- **Chant:** Rhythmic practice of the seven days of the week.

---

## Unit 4 – My birthday party

**Topic:** Dates, months, and party food/drinks.

- **Vocabulary:** _January - December, chips, grapes, lemonade, water, jam, cake._
- **Sentence Patterns:**
- Asking about birthdays: _When's your birthday? -> It's in [Month]._
- Offering/Requesting food: _What do you want to [eat/drink]? -> I want some [food/drink]._

- **Detailed Activities:**
- **Birthday Circle:** Line up in order of birth months without speaking Vietnamese to practice "When's your birthday?".
- **Party Planning:** Create a "Menu" for a dream birthday party including favorite snacks and beverages.
- **Listening:** Listen to guests at a party and tick the food/drinks they request.

---

## Unit 5 – Things we can do

**Topic:** Abilities and skills.

- **Vocabulary:** _swim, sing, dance, cook, cycle, skate, skip, play the piano/guitar._
- **Sentence Patterns:**
- Asking about ability: _Can you [activity]? -> Yes, I can / No, I can't._
- Asking about others: _Can he/she [activity]? -> Yes, he/she can / No, he/she can't._

- **Detailed Activities:**
- **Talent Show Survey:** Survey the class to find out who can perform certain skills (e.g., "Can you play the guitar?").
- **Mime Game:** One student mimes an action (like skating), and the others guess "He can skate!".
- **Writing:** Write a short paragraph about what you can and cannot do using "but" (e.g., "I can swim but I can't dive").

---

## Unit 6 – Our school facilities

**Topic:** School locations and rooms.

- **Vocabulary:** _school, playground, computer room, gym, library, garden, buildings._
- **Sentence Patterns:**
- Asking about school location: _Where's your school? -> It's in the [mountains/village/city/town]._
- Asking about quantity: _How many [rooms/buildings] are there at your school? -> There is one / There are [number]._

- **Detailed Activities:**
- **Map Drawing:** Draw a simple map of the school and label the facilities.
- **Matching:** Match descriptions of activities (e.g., "We read books here") with the correct room (_Library_).

---

## Unit 7 – Our timetables

**Topic:** School subjects.

- **Vocabulary:** _Maths, Vietnamese, English, Science, IT, Music, Art, PE._
- **Sentence Patterns:**
- Asking about subjects: _What subjects do you have today? -> I have [subjects]._
- Asking about frequency: _When do you have [subject]? -> I have it on [Days]._

- **Detailed Activities:**
- **Timetable Comparison:** Compare two different student timetables and find the differences.
- **Listening for Detail:** Listen to a student talking about their school day and fill in the missing subjects in a table.

---

## Unit 8 – My favourite subjects

**Topic:** Preferences and reasons for school subjects.

- **Vocabulary:** _favourite, because, want to be, teacher, singer, scientist, artist._
- **Sentence Patterns:**
- Asking about favorites: _What's your favourite subject? -> It's [subject]._
- Asking why: _Why do you like it? -> Because I want to be a/an [job]._

- **Detailed Activities:**
- **Poster Making:** Create a "My Favourite Subject" poster explaining the reason and future dream job.
- **Group Discussion:** Talk about why certain subjects are useful for different careers.

---

## Unit 9 – Our sports day

**Topic:** School events and months of the year.

- **Vocabulary:** _Sports Day, Teachers' Day, Independence Day, Children's Day, May, June, September, November._
- **Sentence Patterns:**
- Asking about event timing: _When's [Event]? -> It's in [Month]._

- **Detailed Activities:**
- **Event Matching:** Match national holidays with their respective months.
- **Role-play:** Pretend to be an organizer announcing the dates for school events on a megaphone.

---

## Unit 10 – Our summer holidays

**Topic:** Past locations and holiday experiences.

- **Vocabulary:** _at the campsite, on the beach, in the countryside, in the city, London, Tokyo, Bangkok._
- **Sentence Patterns:**
- Asking about the past: _Where were you last summer? -> I was [location]._
- Yes/No questions: _Were you [location]? -> Yes, I was / No, I wasn't._

- **Detailed Activities:**
- **Photo Album:** Look at pictures of various landmarks and guess where the person was.
- **Postcard Writing:** Write a short postcard to a friend describing where you were during the summer break.
